# Still Here

Crisis hope page — faith-grounded resource for people searching for hope in their darkest moments.

## What This Is
A single-page resource for people in crisis. Not a wellness app, not a sermon. A hand in the dark. Meets people in pain, acknowledges it, offers honest hope grounded in scripture, and connects them to real help.

## Architecture
- Pure HTML + CSS, no JavaScript, no dependencies
- GitHub Pages deployment
- Mobile-first, dark warm design ("candlelight")
- Zero tracking, zero cookies, zero data collection

## Design System: "Candlelight"
- Palette: warm brown-blacks, amber/gold accent, forest green for action buttons
- Typography: Georgia serif, 18-20px base, 1.8 line height
- Single column, 640px max, generous spacing
- No animations — designed for someone who may be anxious

## Content Principles
- Acknowledge pain before offering hope (David's Psalms model)
- Authentic, not preachy — faith woven naturally, not prescribed
- Composite stories: honest but not attributed to real individuals
- Always lead with professional crisis resources (988, Crisis Text Line)
- Disclaimer: not a substitute for professional care
